Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert YouTube video to MP3 online for free?

Simply paste your YouTube video URL into the input field, click "Convert to MP3", and wait a few seconds. Once ready, choose your preferred audio quality and download the MP3 file. No registration required!

Is converting YouTube to MP3 legal?

Converting videos for personal use is generally acceptable. However, please respect copyright laws and only convert videos you own or have permission to use. Do not distribute copyrighted content without authorization.

What audio quality options are available?

We offer three quality options: 128 kbps (Standard - smaller file size), 192 kbps (Good - balanced quality), and 320 kbps (High Quality - best audio). Higher quality means larger file size. Choose based on your needs.

How long does conversion take?

Conversion typically takes 10-30 seconds for standard length videos (3-5 minutes). Longer videos (over 10 minutes) may take 30-60 seconds. Processing time depends on video length and our server load.

What YouTube URL formats are supported?

We support all YouTube URL formats: standard (youtube.com/watch?v=...), shortened (youtu.be/...), embed links (youtube.com/embed/...), and YouTube Shorts (youtube.com/shorts/...). Just paste the URL as-is!

Why can't I convert certain videos?

Some videos may be unavailable due to copyright restrictions, age restrictions, regional blocks, or private/unlisted status. Both public and unlisted videos work. Age-restricted videos may not be accessible.

What's the estimated file size for MP3?

Approximate file sizes per minute: 128kbps: ~0.9MB/min, 192kbps: ~1.4MB/min, 320kbps: ~2.4MB/min. For a 4-minute song: 128kbps (~3.6MB), 192kbps (~5.6MB), 320kbps (~9.6MB). Our tool shows estimated size before downloading.
